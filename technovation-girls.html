Technovation Girls is a free, global technology entrepreneurship program for girls aged 8 - 18 that encourages them to identify local problems and build mobile or web apps to solve them, fostering leadership and innovation skills.

At the Maple Valley Community Center, our TechSquid Club hosts the Technovation girls program, where we:

  • Identify a problem in our community we care about
  • Brainstorm and design an innovative technology solution
  • Learn coding skills to build a mobile app or AI prototype
  • Create a business plan and think like entrepreneurs
  • Collaborate in teams and gain guidance from mentors
  • Pitch our project in regional and global competitions
