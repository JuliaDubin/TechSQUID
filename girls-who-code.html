Girls Who Code is a global nonprofit organization working to close the gender gap in technology and computer science. GWC Clubs are free, community-based programs where girls and non-binary students come together to explore coding in a fun, supportive, and inclusive environment.

At the Maple Valley Community Center, our TechSquid Club hosts the GWC program, where we:

  • Learn the basics of coding through hands-on projects
  • Work together to design solutions to real-world problems
  • Build confidence as leaders and innovators in technology
  • Connect with a global sisterhood of students passionate about STEM
