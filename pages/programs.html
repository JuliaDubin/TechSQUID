Exploring tech, one tentacle at a time.

We offer a range of free, community-centered tech programs that inspire curiosity and build confidence.

We embrace mistakes as part of the learning process. By working together to debug and problem-solve, we grow stronger as thinkers, teammates, and creators knowing that every challenge is a step forward.
